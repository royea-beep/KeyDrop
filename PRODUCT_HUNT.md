## First comment from maker (longer pitch)

Hey Product Hunt 👋

We built KeyDrop because we were tired of chasing API keys over WhatsApp, Slack, and email. Screenshots, copy-paste in DMs, and “can you send that again?” — it’s slow and insecure.

KeyDrop fixes that:

1. **You create a request** — Pick a template (Stripe, Facebook, Google, etc.) or define custom fields. Add labels like “API Key” or “Webhook Secret.”

2. **You share one link** — Send it to your client however you want (email, Slack, or yes, even WhatsApp). The link is unique and one-time use.

3. **They submit once** — Your client fills the form on any device. No account needed for them.

4. **You get it encrypted** — Credentials are AES-256 encrypted at rest. Only you can decrypt. The link expires after submission, so no reuse and no leaks.

We’re a small team shipping from Israel. KeyDrop uses LemonSqueezy for billing (so we can sell globally) and works with SQLite for side projects or Postgres for production.

If you’re a freelancer, agency, or DevOps person who ever had to “get the key from the client,” KeyDrop is for you. Try the free tier — 5 requests/month, no card required.

Happy to answer questions and hear your feedback. 🚀

## 5 key features

1. **AES-256 encryption** — Every credential encrypted at rest; keys never stored in plaintext.
2. **One-time links** — Unique link per request; expires after submission.
3. **Service templates** — Pre-built templates for Stripe, Facebook, Google, and more.
4. **No client account** — Clients just open the link and submit; no signup.
5. **Simple pricing** — Free (5 req/mo), Pro $19/mo (100 req), Team $49/mo (unlimited).
